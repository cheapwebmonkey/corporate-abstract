Current Gallery | Gallery 23, Jean Salmon, Artist

I work in the abstract because of the sense of mystery. I never know where a painting will take me, I simply immerse myself in a primal flurry of light, movement and form. It is over when a certain balance has been reached, and the end result feels visually complete to me.
